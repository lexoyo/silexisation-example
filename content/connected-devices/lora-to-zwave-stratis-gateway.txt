LoRa-to-ZWave STRATIS Gateway
Gateways are placed strategically throughout the building
Not in individual apartments
Description
Features

    Small form factor, versatile design fits almost anywhere.
    Plug and play connectivity without opening or drilling into the housing.
    External recessed power, antenna, and Ethernet (PoE).
    Designed with the PoE to leverage a building’s existing network infrastructure.
    Easily and securely commissions in seconds.
    Capable of managing up to 200 enabled devices for a wide range of applications.
    Secure AES-256 bit encrypted 2.4 GHz Bluetooth 4.0 communication between the Artis Gateway enabled devices does not interfere with (802.11a/b/g/n/ac) WI-FI networks.

Tech Specs

    Model Number: Artis Gateway
    Transmission/encryption: AES-256 bit key
    Dimensions: 3.5 x 2.5 x 1
    Power: PoE (12.95W compliant to IEEE 802.3af/at) with supplied adapter. 5VDC.

    Communication Range: Up to 300 ft.
    Protocol: Z-Wave
    Transmission/encryption: TLS version 1.2 or greater (to IP host), 256 bit
